## Consolidation Notes (for AC phase)

Configure Equipment Pool - Equipment Bonus Non-stacking:
Groups the non-stacking rule across all equipment categories. Only the highest bonus of the same type applies.
AC must specify: armor bonus capped at highest; Protection from device stacks (not equipment bonus); bonus type distinctions.

Acquire Weapons - Area Weapons:
Grenades and explosives share Burst/Cloud Area mechanic with Dodge DC.
AC must specify: Burst Area Dodge DC 15 for half; Cloud Area lingers; grenade vs explosive delivery distinction.

Select Exotic Melee Weapon:
Each exotic has unique mechanic beyond Strength-based Damage.
AC must specify unique properties: Chain/whip (Reach + Improved Grab/Trip), Chainsaw (non-Strength fixed rank), Whip (Reach 3).

Build Device - Cost Calculations:
Removable vs Easily Removable and Indestructible interact on same device.
AC must specify: Removable cost reduction; Easily Removable greater reduction; Indestructible reverses some reduction.

Invent Temporary Device - Time Reduction Trade-off:
Reducing design or construction time applies -5 check penalty per time rank reduced.
AC must specify: time rank table for design (1 hr/pp) and construction (4 hr/pp); jury-rigging bypasses this.

Design Vehicle - Shared Feature Pattern with Headquarters:
Vehicle features and HQ features both cost 1 point each; power effects charged at 1/5 normal.
AC must confirm one-fifth cost applies to power effects in vehicles/HQ but not normal equipment items.

Build Construct - Zero Net Cost Invariant:
Construct package (-30 pp absent abilities + 30 pp Immunity to Fortitude) nets to zero.
AC must verify: cannot selectively drop only some absent abilities; always one pair (Intellect+Presence or Strength+Agility).

## Context Gaps

- Construct size variation: source says larger/smaller constructs pay power points for Innate/Permanent Growth or Shrinking but does not specify application adjudication criteria.
- Alternate Equipment hero-point stunt: GM decides whether having an item on-hand is possible; decision criteria unspecified - treat as GM discretion in AC.
- Vehicle multi-mode movement: Alternate Effects for multiple movement modes confirmed; how application handles in-play mode switching not specified.
- Ritual mishap details: 3+ degrees failure on ritual research "may result in mishap" but no mishap table given - cross-reference to GM section needed.
- Team equipment point pooling: source says divide cost "however they wish" - no system enforcement; treat as player/GM agreement in AC.
