Consolidation Notes (for AC phase)

Assign Skill Ranks
Skill rank purchase follows a fixed 2-ranks-per-PP rate. Ranks may be split freely across any skills. The skill modifier limit is enforced by Character Construction at build time, not at check time.

Enforce Skill Modifier Limit
Total skill modifier (rank + ability + misc) may not exceed PL + 10. AC must specify: total modifier calculation, limit value, and handling when limit is exceeded.

Make Skill Check
Core resolution parameterized across all named skills: d20 + rank + ability + circumstance vs. DC. Per-skill stories specify skill-specific DCs and outcomes.

Apply Critical Success on Natural 20
A natural 20 increases the degree of success by one after normal calculation. AC must cover: trigger condition, degree adjustment, and edge case where degree is already maximum.

Resolve Untrained Skill Attempt
Two paths: (1) skills without Trained Only -- attempt proceeds with rank 0, ability modifier applies; (2) Trained Only skills -- automatic failure, no roll.

Apply Skill Mastery Routine Result
Skill Mastery allows routine check result (bonus + 10) even under stress, replacing a required roll for the nominated skill.

Enforce Interaction Skill Requirements
Three pre-conditions: (1) subject awareness/comprehension (-5 if not met), (2) Intellect minimum (-5 for Int -5, blocked for subjects lacking mental abilities), (3) Immunity effect (blocked entirely).

Feint Opponent with Deception
Combat action (standard action), makes target vulnerable until end of user's next round. Distinct from Bluffing.

Intimidate Group of Minions
Single check vs. single GM-rolled group resistance; same effect required for all members.

Detect Illusion Secretly with Insight
GM-initiated secret check (DC 10 + Illusion rank). Player does not decide when to attempt.

Collect Physical Evidence vs. Analyze Collected Evidence
Two-step process: Gather Evidence (DC 15) then Analyze Evidence (DC 15). Failure to gather degrades or prevents analysis.

Add Close Combat / Ranged Combat Rank to Attack Check
Rank adds only to attack checks for the specific weapon/power named. No benefit to defense. Narrow alternative to raising Fighting/Dexterity rank.

Perform Jury-Rig Repair
DC is 10 lower than building, standard action, fixes one problem, lasts until end of scene. Cannot be jury-rigged again until fully repaired.

Revive Dazed or Stunned Character
Removes dazed or stunned only. Does not remove other conditions. Cannot revive a dying character without first stabilizing.
